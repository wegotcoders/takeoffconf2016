"Digital Nomad" != "Location Independent"
So you don't have to backpack around, earning just enough for your next ticket...
We are Lea & Jonathan Woodward, a British couple with two young kids, Mali & Samson. We didn’t think “nomadic” accurately described what we were doing – running our business online from wherever we travelled to – and so we came up with the term, Location Independent*, instead.
“We knew that there were plenty of places in the world where you can live on a lot less than we were earning and have the same, if not a better standard of living. So we asked ourselves: What if we left the UK for one of these places and continued to run our existing businesses?”
